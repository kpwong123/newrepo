Welcome to Fencing Info!

This is a page with all the information you need about fencing.

Why should you learn fencing?

Fencing is a sport that can train your agility and patience. The sport allows you to gain mental and physical strength through exciting combats.

What can you find in this site?

A fencer or not, this site provides all the necessary information about this elegant sport. You will find facts about the essential equiment, fencing techniques and links to local fencing academies in this website. Three types of weapons, namely Sabre, Epee and Foil, will also be introduced in the 'Weapons' section.

Fencing is a perfect sport during COVID-19!

You won't get infected by any means when you are playing fencing!

Three reasons for playing fencing during the outbreak:

  • You wear gloves - Feel free to shake hands with your opponent
  • You wear a mask - Hard for viruses to get through
  • Stab anyone who gets close to you
